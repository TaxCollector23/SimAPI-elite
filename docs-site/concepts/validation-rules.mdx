SimAPI's deterministic engine runs **53 validation layers** totaling **287
checks** across **21 simulation domains**. Only warnings and failures are
surfaced; passing checks are counted but not listed.

## Layer categories

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Input quality" icon="clipboard-check">
    Dataset non-empty, sufficient trials, no duplicate rows, column completeness,
    variance and mode-collapse detection, subnormal-value checks.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Plausibility bounds" icon="arrows-left-right-to-line">
    Every quantity is range-checked against domain-specific physical bounds
    (e.g. drag coefficient ∈ [0.0005, 3.5], Mach ∈ [0, 0.99]).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Conservation & dimensional" icon="scale-balanced">
    Mass/energy balance, dimensional consistency, and unit-conversion artifacts.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Cross-variable physics" icon="diagram-project">
    Relationships between quantities — e.g. Reynolds number vs. velocity and
    viscosity, lift-to-drag consistency, Mach vs. velocity.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Statistical & numerical" icon="chart-line">
    Distribution shape, outlier detection, stationarity, multicollinearity,
    autocorrelation, and numerical-stability signals.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Domain-specific" icon="atom">
    Dedicated layers for aerodynamics, structural, thermodynamics, robotics,
    combustion, acoustics, electromagnetics, nuclear, plasma, and more.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## How the verdict is computed

<Steps>
  <Step title="Run all layers">
    Each layer emits `PhysicsCheck` results tagged `passed`, `warning`, or `failed`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Exclude bad trials">
    Trials that violate hard bounds are excluded, producing an `exclusion_rate`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Aggregate">
    Any failure or `exclusion_rate > 0.5` → **failed**. Any warning or
    `exclusion_rate > 0.2` → **warning**. Otherwise **passed**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set training_ready">
    `true` when not failed, at least 10 valid trials remain, and exclusion rate < 0.5.
  </Step>
</Steps>
