## Secrets

No secrets live in the codebase. All credentials are read from the environment
(see [`.env.example`](https://github.com/TaxCollector23/SimAPI-/blob/main/.env.example)).
CI runs [gitleaks](https://github.com/gitleaks/gitleaks) on every push and a
`detect-private-key` pre-commit hook runs locally.

<Warning>
  The pre-1.0 history contained a hardcoded model-provider key. It has been
  removed and must be treated as compromised — **rotate it** if it was ever live.
</Warning>

## Production hardening checklist

<Steps>
  <Step title="Require auth">Set `SIMAPI_REQUIRE_AUTH=true` and provision `SIMAPI_API_KEYS`.</Step>
  <Step title="Lock down CORS">Restrict `SIMAPI_CORS_ORIGINS` to known origins.</Step>
  <Step title="Terminate TLS">At your load balancer or ingress.</Step>
  <Step title="Ship logs">Keep `SIMAPI_LOG_JSON=true` and forward to a central store.</Step>
  <Step title="Rate limit">Use the built-in limiter (or a shared Redis limiter for multi-replica).</Step>
</Steps>

## Reporting a vulnerability

Email `security@simapi.dev` with a description, reproduction, and impact. Do not
open a public issue. We acknowledge within 48 hours and target a fix within 7
days for high-severity issues. See the full
[security policy](https://github.com/TaxCollector23/SimAPI-/blob/main/SECURITY.md).
